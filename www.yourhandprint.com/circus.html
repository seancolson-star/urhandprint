JOIN US TO CELEBRATE CIRCUS HISTORY!  
 
SATURDAY, OCTOBER 12th marks the 45th Anniversary of The Ringling Bros. and Barnum & Bailey Circus Clown College Reunion. As many as 150 clowns will be coming to Sarasota County from around the world. All are invited to tour the Venice facility, and join us for a luncheon meet-and-greet at the Arena on October 12, 2013 at 12:00 PM. 

Another activity will be to have their hand prints and signatures cast in concrete for the CIRCUS WALK OF FAME.  We will be seeking sponsorships for the cost of the casting. (Appointments for making hand prints must be scheduled in advance by calling 941-485-7675.)  

Created as a fund raiser, the CIRCUS WALK OF FAME is destined to become a tourist attraction and memorial to those who gave so much joy to so many. It’s a chance to nominate those Circus Greats you will always remember! In addition, there will be a SUPPORTER'S WALK designed for those who appreciate our unique circus history and wish to show their support in a very personal way.  

Armando & Tito Gaona of the famous Flying Gaonas trapeze act immortalize their hand prints in pavers for the CIRCUS WALK OF FAME, while Orlando Bevington of the The Venice Circus Arts Foundation, cast his as a supporter of the project.
